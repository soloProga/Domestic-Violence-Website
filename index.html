What is Domestic Abuse?

Domestic abuse can be defined as the use of physical violence or use of psychological and verbal threats, to maintain a dominant position over a spouse, or an intimate partner. Domestic abuse can be occasional or chronic. There are many forms of domestic abuse some entail social abuse, financial abuse, or sexual assault. This type of human degradation does not only occur in one part of the world, but it reaches all corners and affects people from all walks of life.

Why NotifyBB addresses Domestic abuse?

I chose this area to address because it’s one that’s been exerting influence on persons from all types of socioeconomic backgrounds for a very long time, the ramifications are great and have chain effects not only on the person directly affected by it, but on communities and societies on whole

Understanding the problem NotifyBB is solving

Children who witness domestic violence committed in their homes are likely to develop behavioral issues in the future, these behavioral issues stem from repeating the cycle of abuse, anger, anxiety and other mental disorders. Statistics show that 3.3 million to 10 million children in America are affected by domestic violence alone and 1 in 3 women in the Caribbean are Victims. 80% of Domestic abuse victims are hesitant to report abuse due to misinformation, fear of retaliation, dependency and not thoroughly understanding the deleterious situation they themselves are in.
